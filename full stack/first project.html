biography

Aloo Bhujia is a popular Indian snack made from chickpea flour and potatoes, seasoned with spices. Originating from the city of Bikaner in Rajasthan, it was first created by the Bikanervala family in 1960. This crispy, savory treat quickly gained widespread popularity for its unique flavor and versatility. Often enjoyed as a standalone snack, it is also used as a topping for various dishes like chaats and sandwiches. Over the years, Aloo Bhujia has become a staple in Indian households and has a significant presence in international markets, cherished for its delicious taste and crunchy texture.
